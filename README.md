## What You Get: Feature Highlights

**Email builder**: Drag-and-drop editor, 50+ pre-built templates, 500,000+ royalty-free stock photos available right inside the editor, and mobile-responsive designs. HTML editing is available but requires a settings toggle rather than a seamless switch — a minor quirk worth knowing.

**Automation & workflows**: Visual workflow builder with behavioral triggers (email opens, clicks, website visits), time delays, and conditions. Drip sequences run separately from the main workflow builder, which trips some people up at first, but it works well once you get used to the setup.

**List building tools**: Popup forms with exit-intent tech, inline embeddable forms, conversion-focused landing pages, a WordPress plugin, and CSV import/export. A/B testing is available for forms and landing pages too.

**Analytics**: Real-time dashboards, email heatmaps, engagement depth tracking (whether your email was read fully, skimmed, or just briefly opened — actually a neat feature), geolocation data, and device/client breakdowns.

**Deliverability tools**: Automatic IP warm-up, sender reputation monitoring, spam testing before send, full email authentication guidance (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), bounce management, and domain reputation tracking. This is arguably the most developed part of the platform.

**AI features**: An AI writing assistant for email copy, AI image generation, and content suggestions. Not the most powerful AI suite you'll find, but it covers everyday use cases without needing a separate tool.

**Integrations**: 12 native integrations including Shopify, WooCommerce, Stripe, PayPal, Pipedrive, Slack, Intercom, Twilio, Zapier, Instapage, ClickFunnels, and Teachable. Zapier extends this to thousands of additional apps.
